With our extensive knowledge and experience we kept up to date with the latest trends and developments.

Services

CONVEYANCING:

We are an elite firm specialising in property law. We specialise in transfer of properties, which includes Deceased transfers, consolidations, subdivisions, Sectional Developments and Township Registrations.


DECEASED ESTATES AND WILLS:

We assist in drafting of wills as well finalising deceased estates. A deceased estate is a very delicate matter and should be treated as such. You need an expert to lead you through the overwhelming legal requirements, we will assist with the process with the necessary compassion leaving you with a sense of comfort knowing that all administrative requirements are attended to.


CONTRACTS:

We assist in drafting of contracts to your needs. Somewhere in your life you will sign a contract, whether it is to start a partnership or buy a cellphone, it is part of our daily lives. It is important to get professional advice, don’t get caught in the “fine print”.


ANTENUPTIAL CONTRACTS:

Are you getting married? We can assist in drafting of such an antenuptial contract with the highest standard of care.
